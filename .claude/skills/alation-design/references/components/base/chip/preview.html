Chip

A Chip is a compact pill that rides inline with content and labels what it sits next to — a category, a status, a person, an applied filter. Alation code has two Chip wrappers on top of MUI Chip: LabelChip for categorical and status labels (12 semantic colours, no delete), and ObjectChip for naming a person with a leading User icon. Interactive filter / tag chips that need onClick or onDelete fall back to the raw MUI <Chip size="small">. Never hand-paint a chip with appearance sx.
