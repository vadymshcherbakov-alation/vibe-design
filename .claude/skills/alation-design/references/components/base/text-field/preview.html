Text Field

A Text Field is the free-text input primitive. Use it for names, descriptions, emails, passwords, numbers, URLs, and short search queries — wrapped in a Form Field composite at the call site. Choice-from-a-list controls (Select, Autocomplete), booleans (Checkbox), and dates (DatePicker) are siblings, not Text Field variants — see Form Field for the full input-surface list.
