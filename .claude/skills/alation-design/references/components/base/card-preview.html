Card Wrapper

Card Wrapper is the low-level surface primitive that gives other UI its rounded, bordered container. It groups related content into one readable surface — a summary tile, a settings group, a metric, a nav entry — and gives that content a shape the eye can scan as a single unit. Reach for Card Wrapper whenever a block of information needs to sit apart from the page flow as its own readable object. The name distinguishes this base primitive from the composite cards (Nav Card, Metric Card) that are built on top of it.
